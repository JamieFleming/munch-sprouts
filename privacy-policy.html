Last updated: 22/04/2026

User-Generated Data

  • Child profiles (e.g. name, age, date of birth)

Your data is stored securely and only accessible to you via your account.

We do not knowingly collect personal data directly from children. Any child-related data is provided by the parent or guardian and remains under their control.
