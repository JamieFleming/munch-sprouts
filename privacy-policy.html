Privacy Policy for MunchSprout

Last updated: 25/04/2026

MunchSprout (“we”, “our”, or “us”) respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, share, and safeguard your information when you use the MunchSprout website and mobile application, including related services, content, and features.

We do not sell your personal data. We only collect and use personal data where we have a valid legal reason to do so and where it is necessary to provide MunchSprout, improve it, keep it secure, and respond to support requests.

Who We Are

MunchSprout is a website and app designed for parents and caregivers to record and manage weaning and feeding information.

For the purposes of UK data protection law, MunchSprout is the data controller for the personal data described in this Privacy Policy.

Information We Collect

Information you provide directly

  • Email address (for account creation, login, and support)
  • Name or display name associated with your account
  • Profile images or other images you choose to upload
  • Messages or information you send when contacting us

User-generated app data

  • Child profiles (for example name, age, or date of birth)
  • Food logs, dates, reactions, categories, favourites, and preferences
  • Notes, feeding history, and other records you choose to add
  • Recipes or other content submitted through the app or website

Technical and usage information

  • Device type, browser type, and operating system
  • Basic log, diagnostic, crash, and performance information
  • App and website usage information needed to maintain and improve the service

How We Use Your Information

  • Provide, operate, and maintain MunchSprout
  • Create and manage your account
  • Sync your information securely across devices
  • Enable features such as dashboards, image upload, and exports
  • Respond to support requests and other enquiries
  • Improve app performance, reliability, and user experience
  • Monitor, prevent, and investigate misuse, fraud, or security issues
  • Comply with legal obligations and enforce our terms where necessary

We do not sell your personal data.

Lawful Bases for Processing

Depending on how you use MunchSprout, we rely on one or more lawful bases under UK data protection law, including performance of a contract where we provide the service you request, legitimate interests in running, improving, and securing MunchSprout, legal obligations where processing is required by law, and consent where consent is specifically requested.

Where we rely on legitimate interests, these interests generally include operating the platform effectively, improving features, preventing abuse, and keeping user data and systems secure.

Data Storage & Security

  • Secure authentication systems
  • Encrypted data transmission where supported
  • Restricted access to stored data
  • Cloud storage and infrastructure providers used to host and process data

Your data is stored securely and only accessible to you through your account, subject to the technical and organisational measures used to protect the service. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, so absolute security cannot be guaranteed.

Data Sync & Accounts

  • Your data may be stored securely in the cloud to enable account access
  • You may be able to access your data across multiple devices
  • You are responsible for keeping your login details secure
  • You should notify us promptly if you believe your account has been accessed without permission

Data Sharing

  • With service providers who help us operate the app or website, such as hosting, storage, authentication, analytics, or support tools
  • When required by law, regulation, court order, or legal process
  • To protect rights, safety, property, users, or the integrity of the service
  • In connection with a business transfer, merger, sale, or restructuring, if that ever occurs

We require service providers to process personal data only as needed to provide services to us and in accordance with appropriate privacy and security obligations.

International Transfers

Some of the service providers we use may process personal data outside the UK. Where this happens, appropriate safeguards will be used where required by law, such as adequacy regulations or contractual protections designed to safeguard personal data.

Children’s Privacy

MunchSprout is designed for use by parents and caregivers.

We do not knowingly collect personal data directly from children. Any child-related data is entered by a parent or guardian and remains under their control. Users should only enter information they are entitled to provide.

Cookies and Similar Technologies

The MunchSprout website may use cookies or similar technologies that are necessary for the operation, security, performance, and improvement of the website. If non-essential cookies or similar technologies are used, they should only be activated in accordance with applicable consent requirements.

Your Rights

  • Request access to personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Request restriction of processing in certain circumstances
  • Object to certain processing where applicable
  • Request portability of data where applicable
  • Withdraw consent at any time where processing is based on consent

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at:
📧 munchsprouts@outlook.com

Data Retention

We retain personal data only for as long as necessary for the purposes described in this Privacy Policy, including to provide the service, maintain records, resolve disputes, enforce agreements, and comply with legal obligations. If you delete your account or request deletion, your personal data will be deleted or anonymised within a reasonable timeframe unless we are required or permitted to retain certain information by law.

Subscriptions and Billing

MunchSprouts offers auto-renewable subscription plans. Subscriptions are purchased through the Apple App Store or Google Play Store. Payment is charged to your App Store or Google Play account at confirmation of purchase. Subscriptions automatically renew unless cancelled at least 24 hours before the end of the current period. You can manage or cancel your subscription at any time through your device's account settings. We do not have access to your payment card details — all billing is handled by Apple or Google.

Third-Party Links and Services

The website or app may contain links to third-party websites, platforms, or services. We are not responsible for the privacy practices, content, or security of third-party services, and users should review their privacy policies separately.

Complaints

If you have concerns about how your personal data is handled, please contact us first so we can try to resolve the issue. You also have the right to lodge a complaint with the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO) in the UK.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted within the app, on the website, or both. The “Last updated” date at the top of this page shows when this Privacy Policy was most recently revised.

Contact Us

If you have any questions, requests, or concerns about this Privacy Policy or your personal data, contact us at:
📧 munchsprouts@outlook.com
