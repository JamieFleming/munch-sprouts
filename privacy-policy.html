Privacy Policy

Last updated: 14 May 2026

MunchSprouts ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, share, and safeguard your information when you use the MunchSprouts website, mobile application, and related services.

We do not sell your personal data.

1. Who We Are

MunchSprouts is a website and mobile application designed for parents and caregivers to record and manage baby feeding and weaning information.

For the purposes of UK data protection law, MunchSprouts is the data controller for the personal data described in this Privacy Policy.

Contact: 📧 munchsprouts@outlook.com

2. Information We Collect

Information you provide directly

  • Email address
  • Display name or account name
  • Uploaded profile images or photos
  • Messages or support enquiries

User-generated app data

  • Child profiles
  • Feeding records
  • Food logs
  • Allergens and reactions
  • Notes and preferences
  • Uploaded images and memory photos
  • Recipes or submitted content

Technical & usage information

  • Device type
  • Operating system
  • Browser type
  • Diagnostic and crash data
  • Usage analytics and performance information

While some information entered into the app may relate to feeding, allergens, or reactions, MunchSprouts is not intended to collect or process medical records or healthcare data.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use personal data to:

  • Provide and maintain the service
  • Create and manage accounts
  • Sync data across devices
  • Enable app features and exports
  • Respond to support requests
  • Improve app reliability and performance
  • Prevent misuse, fraud, or security issues
  • Comply with legal obligations

We do not sell personal data.

4. Lawful Bases for Processing

Depending on how you use MunchSprouts, we rely on:

  • Performance of a contract — to provide the service you've signed up for
  • Legitimate interests — to operate, improve, and secure the service
  • Legal obligations — where processing is required by law
  • Consent — where specifically requested

Where we rely on legitimate interests, these include operating and improving the service, preventing abuse, and securing systems and user data.

5. Data Storage & Security

We use reasonable technical and organisational measures to help protect personal data, including:

  • Secure authentication systems
  • Encrypted transmission where supported
  • Restricted access to stored data
  • Reputable cloud infrastructure providers

Uploaded photos and user content are stored privately and are only accessible to authorised users on the associated account unless explicitly shared by the user.

While we use reasonable safeguards, no method of storage or transmission is completely secure and absolute security cannot be guaranteed.

6. Data Sharing

We may share personal data:

  • With trusted service providers used for hosting, authentication, analytics, storage, or support (including Firebase/Google, RevenueCat, Apple, and Google)
  • Where required by law or legal process
  • To protect rights, users, systems, or service integrity
  • In connection with a merger, acquisition, or business transfer

We require third-party providers to process data only as necessary and subject to appropriate privacy obligations.

7. International Transfers

Some service providers may process personal data outside the UK. Where required, we use appropriate safeguards such as adequacy regulations, contractual protections, or standard data protection clauses.

8. Children's Privacy

MunchSprouts is intended for use by parents and caregivers.

We do not knowingly collect personal data directly from children. Any child-related data is entered and controlled by a parent or guardian. Users are responsible for ensuring they have the right to upload any information, photos, or content entered into the service.

9. Cookies & Similar Technologies

The MunchSprouts website may use cookies or similar technologies necessary for security, functionality, performance, and analytics. Where required by law, consent will be requested before non-essential cookies are used.

10. Your Rights

Subject to applicable law, you may have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your account and data
  • Restrict processing
  • Object to certain processing
  • Request portability of data
  • Withdraw consent where applicable

To exercise your rights: 📧 munchsprouts@outlook.com

11. Automated Decision-Making

MunchSprouts does not use automated decision-making or profiling that produces legal or similarly significant effects on users. All decisions relating to your account are made by humans.

12. Data Retention

We retain personal data only as long as reasonably necessary for providing the service, maintaining records, resolving disputes, enforcing agreements, and complying with legal obligations.

If you delete your account or request deletion, personal data will be deleted or anonymised within a reasonable timeframe unless retention is legally required.

13. Subscriptions & Billing

Subscriptions are purchased through Apple App Store or Google Play Store. Payments are handled entirely by Apple or Google. We do not have access to payment card details.

Subscriptions automatically renew unless cancelled before renewal through your device account settings.

14. Third-Party Links & Services

The app or website may contain links to third-party websites or services. We are not responsible for the privacy, security, or content practices of third-party services. Users should review third-party privacy policies separately.

15. Complaints

If you have concerns about how personal data is handled, please contact us first so we can attempt to resolve the issue.

You also have the right to lodge a complaint with the UK Information Commissioner's Office (ICO) at ico.org.uk.

16. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates may be posted within the app, on the website, or via email where appropriate. Continued use of MunchSprouts after updates take effect constitutes acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.
