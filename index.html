What is Baby Led Weaning

Baby Led Weaning (BLW) is an approach to introduce solid foods where babies feed themselves from the very start, rather than being spoon-fed purees. This method encourages babies to explore food at their own pace, encouraging independence and helping them develop healthy eating habits.

Why Choose Baby Led Weaning?

Develops Independence

Babies learn to self-feed and explore foods at their own pace

Improves Motor Skills

Hand-eye coordination and fine motor skills are developed through self feeding

Easier Transition

Seamlessly join family meals without preparing separate meals for your baby

Less Picky Eating

Exposure to a variety of foods early on helps reduce picky eating habits

Ready to Begin?

1

Check for readiness signs

Ensure your baby shows signs of readiness (usually around 6 months)

2

Learn the basics

Understand food preparation safety, and feeding techniques

3

Start with simple foods

Begin with soft, easy-to-grasp foods and gradually expand your baby's diet as they grow and develop
