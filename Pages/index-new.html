What is Baby Led Weaning

Baby Led Weaning (BLW) is an approach to introduce solid foods where babies feed themselves from the very start, rather than being spoon-fed purees. This method encourages babies to explore food at their own pace, encouraging independence and helping them develop healthy eating habits.
